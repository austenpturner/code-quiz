Coding Quiz Challenge

This quiz will test your basic understanding of HTML, CSS and JavaScript. The quiz will be timed. You will earn a higher score if you answer questions quickly and correctly. Time will be subtracted for each incorrect answer. Your final score will be calculated based on the time remaining when you finish the quiz. After the quiz is over you can enter your initials to save your score. Saved scores can be found on the highscores page. When you are ready, click below to start the quiz.
